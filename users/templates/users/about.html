Who Are We?

Tix A Seat is a ticketing system platform for live entertainment. We care about the fans, artists, and venues. Our goal is to provide an effortless experience to the fans and make shows have seamless access to their audiences. We make it easy for fans to buy tickets and attend events through industry-first features like Rating Score, our event experience platform and User be Seller, the first return policy offered by a major ticketer. These features and more will make us the highest rated and most trusted ticketer. We aim to simplify and modernize the ticketing industry.

Innovative ticketing technology

Our website is a ticketing system that allows users to be both buyers and sellers. Additionally, users can compare the seats in the venue by our Rating Score which is computed by our system.
